Oatmeal

Description

Take half of a cup of oats, a scoop or 2 of flavored protein powder, pinch of salt, and a cup of water and mix in a bowl. Microwave for 1 minute and twenty seconds, then take out and let cool. Now add some toppings! I really like to add peanut butter, if the oatmeal is still warm it will melt the peanut butter and make it have a nice texture. I also like to add frozen fruits like blueberries and strawberries. Then I like to add vanilla greek yogurt, some cut up bananas, and if im feeling extra crazy a little drizzle of honey on top.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Protein powder
  • Water
  • Pinch of salt
  • Peanut butter
  • Various fruits
  • Yogurt
  • Honey
